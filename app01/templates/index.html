Welcome

The original homepage of our group is Here.
Go to EDISON project page and find more online simulation tools.

Our laboratory focuses on (1) the understanding of novel properties of functional nano-materials and (2) the exploration of design rules upon which these materials can be realized into advanced electronics, energy, and bio devices. To achieve these goals, we utilize multi-scale computer modeling and simulation tools. Futhermore, our group devotes significant effort in developing simulation theory and programs.
